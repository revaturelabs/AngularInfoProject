Databinding Definitions

Why Databinding?

Without a framework, one would be responsible for pushign data values into the HTML controls and turning user responses into actions and value updates. Without a framework, writing this logic is tedious and error-prone.
Angular's data-binding mechanism provides a way of coordinating parts of a template with parts of a component. The binding markup to the template html to tell angular how to connect the both sides
